How It Works

Our image compression tool uses modern browser technologies to process your images locally, without uploading them to any server. Here's the simple process:

  1. Upload your image - Drag and drop or click to select files from your device.
  2. Adjust settings - Choose compression level and output format.
  3. Compress - Click the compress button to process your image.
  4. Download - Save the optimized image to your device.

We support all major image formats including JPEG, PNG, and WebP. The tool automatically selects the best format based on your image content and quality settings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, our image compression tool is completely free to use with no hidden charges or watermarks.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all processing happens in your browser. We never upload your images to any server, ensuring complete privacy.

What's the maximum image size I can compress?

The tool can handle most images, but very large files (over 20MB) might cause performance issues on some devices.

Which format should I choose for the best compression?

WebP generally offers the best compression with good quality. Our "Auto" setting will choose the optimal format based on your image.

Can I compress multiple images at once?

Yes, you can select multiple files when uploading, and the tool will process them one by one.
